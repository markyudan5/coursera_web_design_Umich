What's a voice channel?

CCAV plays a lot of online games, so instant communication is critical but FUN. So we provide two popular team voice options:

  • Teamspeak3
  • Discord

To use Teamspeak3, you need to install Teamspeak3 client first. Then connect to our server: 111.111.111.111. Once connected, you may not be able to enter specific channels until an administrator gives you permission. This is recommanded for hardcore players.

Discord is a more light but popular software right now. Client is not required, members can access to the voice channel on most platforms(IOS, Android, Win or Mac) by clicking a URL. This is recommanded for visitors or fast access.
