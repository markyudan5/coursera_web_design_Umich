Background

CCAV is a clan originated from Starcraft2 NA server. But it thrives in the game World of Tanks. Now we also play other popular games like Overwatch, Heroes of the Storm, or other popular games on Steam.

We have a lot talented players with average WN8 2500+ in World of Tanks in our clan. We also have starcraft players in master league or top 500 in Overwatch. For World of Tanks, a lot players from our clan are now in super-unicom clans like Bulba, Mahou, etc

Our primary language is Chinese but English is viable. We use both Teamspeak3 and Discord as voice channel for different purposes.
