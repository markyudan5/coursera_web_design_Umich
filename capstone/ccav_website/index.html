About CCAV

CCAV is a clan originated from Starcraft 2 NA server. But it thrives in the game World of Tanks. Now we also play other popular games like Overwatch, Heroes of the Storm, or other popular games on Steam.

We have a lot of talented players with average WN8 2500+ in World of Tanks in our clan. We also have starcraft players in master league or top 500 in Overwatch. For World of Tanks, most players from our clan are now in super-unicum clans like Bulba, Mahou, etc.
